About

American Standards was a chaotic hardcore, mathcore, and metalcore band from Phoenix, Arizona (2011–2025). Known for explosive live shows, personal lyrics, and a DIY ethos, the band blended the aggression of metal with the urgency of punk.

Across the U.S., they shared stages with artists including Every Time I Die, Norma Jean, Knocked Loose, Comeback Kid, Stick To Your Guns, Atreyu, Zao, and He Is Legend, while remaining rooted in Arizona’s scene.

Their discography spans Still Life (2012), The Death of Rhythm and Blues (2013), Hungry Hands (2014), Anti-Melody (2017), Dopamine Dealer (2023), and final album Future Orphans (2025).

About

American Standards were a chaotic hardcore/mathcore band from Phoenix, Arizona (2011–2025). Blending metallic hardcore aggression with punk urgency and metalcore precision, the band became known for explosive live shows, raw personal lyrics, and a relentless DIY ethos. Their final LP, Future Orphans (2025), cements their role as a cornerstone of Arizona’s heavy underground.
